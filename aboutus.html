Our Services

Comprehensive solar power solutions tailored for all

DCR Solar Power Solutions

Our Domestic Content Requirement (DCR) solar solutions use locally manufactured solar modules and components, aligning with India's "Make in India" initiative. These projects are ideal for customers seeking government subsidies under schemes like PM Suryaghar Free Electricity Scheme or KUSUM Scheme.

Why Choose DCR?

  • Compliant with government regulations for local sourcing.
  • Eligible for subsidies and incentives through programs like the National Solar Mission.
  • Supports local manufacturing and sustainable economic growth.

Non-DCR Solar Power Solutions

Our Non-Domestic Content Requirement (Non-DCR) solar solutions leverage high-efficiency, globally sourced components to deliver cost-effective and scalable solar installations. Perfect for large-scale projects like utility-scale solar farms or commercial setups where cost and efficiency are priorities.

Why Choose Non-DCR?

  • Cost-effective with globally sourced, high-performance equipment.
  • Ideal for projects without mandatory local sourcing requirements.
  • Focuses on rapid deployment and maximum energy output.
