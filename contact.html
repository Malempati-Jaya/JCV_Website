Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about solar energy

How much can I save with solar?

Savings depend on your electricity usage, system size, and location. On average, our customers save 50-80% on their electricity bills annually. Use our Solar Savings Calculator for a personalized estimate.

What is the installation process like?

Our process includes a free consultation, custom design, permitting, professional installation, and system activation. We handle all details to ensure a seamless experience.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible financing plans, including zero-down options and low-interest loans, to make solar accessible for everyone.

What warranties do you provide?

We offer a 25-year warranty on solar panels and inverters, plus a 10-year workmanship warranty, ensuring peace of mind for your investment.
